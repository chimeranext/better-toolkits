# Mensarius Oath Adoption

Guía al studio/fund en la adopción opcional del **Mensarius Oath** — código ético de
govclab para profesionales de inversión.

## Regla de idioma

Español.

## Directorio de salida

```
./portfolio/{studio-name}/
└── mensarius-oath-commitment.md
```

---

## ¿Qué es el Mensarius Oath?

"Mensarius" viene del latín — referencia a los **mensarii**, banqueros/money changers
en la antigua Roma que operaban bajo reglas éticas específicas.

El oath moderno (govclab):

> "I will act with integrity and transparency in my dealings. I will not engage in
> conflicts of interest without disclosure. I will respect the confidentiality of
> entrepreneurs' information. I will not solicit investments I would not make myself.
> I commit to these principles knowing they bind my conduct in all professional
> dealings."

(Paraphrased; see govclab for authoritative text.)

---

## ¿Por qué adoptarlo?

**Pros**:
- Differentiation en crowded VC market ("we hold ourselves to ethical standard")
- Signal a founders (many prefer working con ethical investors)
- Community marker (part of Mensarius-committed fund network)
- Prevention tool (explicit commitments > vague values)

**Cons**:
- Binding — violations damage reputation
- Subjective enforcement (who decides if you violated?)
- Marketing risk if adopted cynically

## Flujo del skill

### Paso 1 — Assessment de fit

**MO-1**: "¿Este studio/fund valora:
- [ ] Transparencia sobre performance (incluso malos trimestres)
- [ ] Conflict of interest disclosure
- [ ] Founder-friendly term sheets
- [ ] Zero-tolerance para misrepresentation
- [ ] Skin in the game (invertir personalmente en funds que recomiendas)

Si marcaste 4-5 → Mensarius oath fits. Si <3 → not a natural fit, adopción puede sonar
cínica."

### Paso 2 — Review del oath

**MO-2**: "Leer el Mensarius Oath completo (govclab resources). Preguntar:
- ¿Hay cláusulas que no puedo honestly commitir?
- ¿Mi operating model tiene conflicts that would violate?
- ¿Mis GPs están on board (no solo yo)?

Si hay dudas: NO adopt. Better honest non-adoption que cynical adoption."

### Paso 3 — Commitment público (opcional)

**MO-3**: "Si se adopta:
- Sign the oath (govclab process)
- Publicar en website del studio / fund
- Communicate a LPs
- Reference en LP updates + reports"

## Principios clave

- **NO adopt cynically**: peor que no adopt
- **Team buy-in necesario**: no solo GP lead
- **Skin in the game**: oath sin consequences es performative
- **Review annually**: pretending to hold ethics es degrading to real ethics
